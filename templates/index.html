Intro and Background

The Democratic Republic of Congo is located in the south west area of Africa. It is mostly composed of rainforests, plains, and valleys. It has a population of nearly 80 million people and is considered one of the most resource rich countries in the world. However these resources have enticed more powerful countries to influence and control the DRC. This is compounded with ethnic based conflicts and wars that the Congo has faced to make it one of the most dangerous countries in the world.
